## Problem Statement
Ice Cream Shop: "Make a Scoop"
- Some customers want to order from home, but the shop doesn't have an ecommerce system.
- There is no clear explanation about the ingredients.
- Most ice cream shops cannot customize flavors or toppings as customers want.

## Solution(s)
- Create an e-commerce platform so customers can order online from anywhere in the country.
- Show the story and ingredient details for every menu.
- Create a “Build Your Own Scoop” system so customers can choose their own base of ice cream, flavor, and toppings.

## My Ecommerce Project
"Make a Scoop" is an e-commerce website where customers can:
- buy ice cream online, cold delivery from the kitchen to the customer.
- view all ice cream flavour menus with ingredient details. 
- customize their own ice cream.
- online payment system.
